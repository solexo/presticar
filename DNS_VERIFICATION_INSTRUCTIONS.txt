GOOGLE SITE VERIFICATION TXT RECORD INSTRUCTIONS
==============================================

To complete the site verification for taxiparis.org, you need to add a TXT record at your DNS provider.

TXT Record Details:
------------------
Name/Host: @
Value: google-site-verification=FQHfaCsIKGKiJf9H5DVKjcWIU80d-2GDNvEj4xYXhXs

Instructions:
------------
1. Log into your domain registrar control panel (where you bought taxiparis.org)
2. Go to DNS Management or DNS Settings
3. Add a new TXT record with:
   - Name/Host: @ (or leave blank, or put taxiparis.org)
   - Value: google-site-verification=FQHfaCsIKGKiJf9H5DVKjcWIU80d-2GDNvEj4xYXhXs
   - TTL: 3600 (or default)

4. Save the changes
5. Wait 15 minutes to 24 hours for DNS propagation
6. Return to Google Search Console and click "Verify" again

Note: The HTML meta tag has also been added to your website as a backup verification method.
