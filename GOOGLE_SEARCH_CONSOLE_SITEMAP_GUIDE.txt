GOOGLE SEARCH CONSOLE - SITEMAP SUBMISSION GUIDE
==================================================

To submit your sitemap to Google Search Console:

STEP 1: Access Google Search Console
-----------------------------------
1. Go to https://search.google.com/search-console/
2. Sign in with your Google account
3. Select your taxiparis.org property

STEP 2: Submit Sitemap
---------------------
1. In the left sidebar, click "Sitemaps"
2. Click "Add a new sitemap" (red button)
3. Enter: sitemap.xml
4. Click "Submit"

STEP 3: Verify Submission
-------------------------
1. After submission, the sitemap will appear in the list
2. Status should show "Success" (green checkmark)
3. You can click on the sitemap to see details:
   - Number of URLs discovered
   - Number of URLs indexed
   - Any errors or warnings

STEP 4: Monitor Progress
-----------------------
- Check regularly for indexing status
- Google may take 24-48 hours to process all URLs
- Monitor for any crawl errors in the "Coverage" section

YOUR SITEMAP URL:
================
https://taxiparis.org/sitemap.xml

ADDITIONAL STEPS FOR BETTER SEO:
================================

1. **Request Indexing**: Use the URL Inspection tool in Search Console to request indexing of important pages

2. **Monitor Performance**: Check the "Performance" tab for search queries and click-through rates

3. **Submit New URLs**: As you create new pages, add them to your sitemap and submit again

4. **Check Mobile Usability**: Use the "Mobile Usability" report to ensure your site works well on mobile devices

IMPORTANT NOTES:
===============
- The sitemap contains 20+ optimized pages for taxi services in Paris
- High-priority pages like airport transfers will be crawled first
- The sitemap includes all major taxi service keywords: Paris, CDG, Orly, 24h service
- Regular sitemap updates will help Google discover new content faster

STATUS CHECK:
============
After submitting, you should see:
✅ Sitemap submitted successfully
✅ No errors in the sitemap report
✅ URLs being discovered and indexed over time

If you encounter any issues, check the "Sitemaps" report for specific error messages.
